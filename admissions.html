Admission Requirements

Admission Requirements

BECE Qualification

Applicants must have completed the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE).

CSSPS Placement

Students should be placed through the Computerised School Selection and Placement System (CSSPS).

Reporting

Applicants must report with all required admission documents and items.

Required Admission Documents

Placement Form

Original CSSPS Placement Form or Admission Letter.

Birth Certificate

Original Birth Certificate or a certified copy.

Passport Photographs

Recent passport-sized photographs for school records.

Medical Information

Any relevant medical information or documents required by the school.

Day & Boarding Students

Kwabeng Anglican Senior High Technical School admits both Day and Boarding students. Students are expected to maintain high standards of discipline, respect school rules, and participate fully in academic and co-curricular activities.

Admission Guidelines

Accept Your Placement

Accept your placement through the official CSSPS process before reporting.

Report on Time

Report to school on the official reopening date stated in your admission letter.

Follow School Rules

Every student must observe the rules and regulations of KASHTS throughout their stay.
