About KASHTS

Kwabeng Anglican Senior High Technical School (KASHTS) is a public mixed co-educational institution located in Kwabeng, Atiwa District, Eastern Region, Ghana. The school is committed to quality education, discipline and preparing students for a successful future.

VISION

A centre of excellence for moulding students to become useful and upright citizens.

MISSION

To produce responsible citizens through effective teaching and training.

CORE VALUES

Teamwork, Excellence, Godliness, Integrity and Quality.

Academic Programs

General Science

Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Elective Mathematics.

General Arts

Government, History, Geography and Literature.

Business

Accounting, Economics and Business Management.

Technical

Building, Electricity, Auto Mechanics and Drawing.

Home Economics

Food, Clothing and Management in Living.

Visual Arts

Graphic Design, Ceramics and Picture Making.
