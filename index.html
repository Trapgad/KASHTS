About KASHTIS

Kwabeng Anglican Senior High Technical School is a government-assisted, mixed co-educational boarding and day school located in Kwabeng within the Atiwa District of the Eastern Region of Ghana.

Our History: The institution was established in 1984 by the Koforidua Diocese of the Anglican Church as a private Technical Institution. Recognizing its impact and growth, the school was officially absorbed into the public Ghanaian education system in 1993.

VISION

A centre of excellence for moulding character of students to become useful and upright in society

MISSION

To produce literates, responsible citizens capable of learning and applying academic and social skills successfully in any setting, through effective teaching and training

CORE VALUES

Teamwork, Excellence, Godliness, Integrity, Quality

Academic Programs Offered

Technical

Applied Electricity, Auto Mechanics, Building Construction, Woodwork, and Engineering Tech.

Agricultural Science

General Agriculture, Animal Husbandry, Crop Husbandry, and Chemistry.

General Science

Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Elective Mathematics.

General Arts

Literature-in-English, History, Government, Geography, and Christian Religious Studies.

Business

Financial Accounting, Cost Accounting, Business Management, and Economics.

Home Economics

Management in Living, Foods and Nutrition, Clothing and Textiles.

Visual Arts

Graphic Design, Picture Making, Ceramics, and General Knowledge in Art.

School Facilities

  • 26 Academic Classrooms for structured lessons.
  • 3 Science Laboratories fully customized for practical physics, chemistry, and biology setups.
  • Computer Laboratory equipped for modern ICT development.
  • Agricultural Assets: Dedicated school fish pond structure.
  • Utility Infrastructure: Independent mechanized borehole water filtration systems.

Historic Achievements

  • 🏆 Academic Excellence: Achieved an elite 100% path metric performance rating in 2012.
  • 🏆 Academic Standing: Recorded its benchmark historically best student aggregate score of 8 in 2011.
  • ⚽ Sports: 1st Position Champions in the Kibi Zone Soccer Competition (2014).
  • 🏐 Athletics: 1st Position Champions in the Kibi Zone Volleyball Tournament (2012).
