About KASHTS

Kwabeng Anglican Senior High Technical School (KASHTS) is a public mixed co-educational institution located in Kwabeng in the Atiwa District of the Eastern Region of Ghana. The school is committed to providing quality education, developing responsible citizens, and preparing students for a bright technological future.

VISION

A centre of excellence for moulding character of students to become useful and upright in society.

MISSION

To produce literates, responsible citizens capable of learning and applying academic and social skills successfully.

CORE VALUES

Teamwork, Excellence, Godliness, Integrity, Quality.

Academic Programs Offered

Technical Program

Applied Electricity, Auto Mechanics, Building Construction and Technical Drawing.

General Science

Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Elective Mathematics.

General Arts

Literature, History, Government, Geography and CRS.

Business

Accounting, Business Management and Economics.

Home Economics

Foods and Nutrition, Clothing and Textiles.

Visual Arts

Graphic Design, Ceramics and Picture Making.
