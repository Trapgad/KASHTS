About KASHTS

Kwabeng Anglican Senior High Technical School is a government-assisted mixed co-educational boarding and day school located in Kwabeng, Atiwa District, Eastern Region of Ghana.

History: The institution was established in 1984 by the Koforidua Diocese of the Anglican Church and later absorbed into the public Ghanaian education system in 1993.

VISION

A centre of excellence for moulding students to become useful and upright members of society.

MISSION

To produce responsible citizens through effective teaching, training and discipline.

CORE VALUES

Teamwork, Excellence, Godliness, Integrity and Quality.
