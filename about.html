About KASHTS

Knowledge • Discipline • Excellence

Our Story

Kwabeng Anglican Senior High Technical School (KASHTS) is dedicated to academic excellence, technical skills development, discipline and character building.

The school continues to prepare students with knowledge, skills and values needed to become responsible citizens and future leaders.

Vision

A centre of excellence for moulding character of students to become useful and upright in society.

Mission

To produce literates, responsible citizens capable of learning and applying academic and social skills successfully in any setting, through effective teaching and training.

Core Values

• Teamwork
• Excellence
• Godliness
• Integrity
• Quality
