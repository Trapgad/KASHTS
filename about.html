School History

Kwabeng Anglican Senior High Technical School (KASHTS) was established in 1984 in Kwabeng, Atiwa District, Eastern Region of Ghana.

The school operates under the Ghana Education Service and is affiliated with the Anglican Church. It is a Category B Senior High Technical School offering both Day and Boarding facilities.

Over the years, KASHTS has grown into a respected institution known for academic discipline, technical education, and strong moral values.
